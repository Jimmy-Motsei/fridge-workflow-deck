Plain Language Summary – Heads of Agreement

This is a simplified overview of the HoA between Aurellius Group (Pty) Ltd and [Client].

What We’re Building

  • A WhatsApp-based workflow that captures leads, qualifies them, and sends quotes.
  • Includes QR landing pages, approval workflows, broadcasts, analytics, and an admin inbox.
  • Phase 2 adds Setswana language support.

Who Does What

Provider (Aurellius):

  • Builds and funds the pilot.
  • Hosts and maintains the system during trial.
  • Provides dashboards, inbox, analytics, and training.

Client:

  • Supplies WhatsApp number, assets, and approvals.
  • Prints and deploys QR codes.
  • Pays Meta messaging fees and other direct costs.
  • Ensures POPIA compliance inputs.

Money

  • No Build Fee: Aurellius carries build costs.
  • Subscription Fees: Decided before trial ends, based on usage.
  • Pass-Through Costs: Client pays Meta fees, QR printing, translation costs.
  • VAT applies.

  • Trial Period: 2 months from Go-Live
  • Trial End: [Insert Date]

Legal Assurances

  • Each party keeps its own intellectual property.
  • Client gets a licence to use the system for business after fees are paid.
  • Provider not responsible for Meta approval delays.
  • Liability is capped at 12 months’ subscription fees.
  • Confidentiality survives for 5 years.
  • Either party can terminate for material breach (10 days’ cure).

Support

  • During trial: business hours only.
  • P1 issues (system down): 4-hour response target.
  • Post-trial: full SLA to be agreed.
